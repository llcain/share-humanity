Summary

The Nigerian people are in a humanitarian crisis. A conflict between the government and the militant groups have caused millions of people to live as as refugees without access to vital resources such as adequate shelter, clean drinking water, food, and medical care. These people are now stricken with illness and malnurishment and have no place to go.
